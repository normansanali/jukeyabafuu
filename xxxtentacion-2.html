Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfroy, a.k.a XXXTentacion, was an American Rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. XXXTentacion’s net worth was $5 million at the time of his death in June 2018. He accumulated his net worth through his meteoric elevation in the music industry, which began on SoundCloud and continued with a hit album and sold-out performances on tours.

He was also known by the names X, XXX, Jah, and Young Dagger Dick. He worked on the genres Emo rap, trap, lo-fi, scream rap, SoundCloud rap, hip hop, alternative rock, and alternative R&B. He also used to play the vocals. He, though, was a controversial figure in the industry. Still, he was loved by his fans, and his death was extremely sad for people worldwide.

He was active in the industry from 2013 to 2018 and worked with some famous labels, including Bad Vibes Forever, Caroline, EMPIRE, and Capitol. He had his own self-titled YouTube channel and did associated acts with a lot of celebrities, including Members Only, Denzel Curry, Joey Badass, Ronny J, Ski Mask the Slump God, Trippie Redd, and many more. He also knows how to play the piano and guitar.

XXXTentacion, a prominent character in the hip-hop community, used atypical hip-hop techniques and styles throughout his work. The Spanish word Tentacion, translated in English as “temptation,” is the inspiration for his stage moniker, XXXTentacion. His music was a fusion of hip-hop and emo.

Death

XXXTentacion died on 18 June 2018 in Deerfield Beach, Florida, the U.S., due to multiple gunshot wounds on the throat. He was leaving the Riva Motorsports motorcycle dealership when his car was blocked by a black S.U.V. in the parking lot itself. Two armed men approached him, took the Louis Vuitton bag having cash worth $ 50,000 in it, and killed him. He was later taken to the Broward Health North Hospital, where he was announced dead. The accused, Michael Boatwright, who pulled the trigger, Dedrick Devonshay, and a few others were arrested by the police a few days after his murder. The criminals were charged with first-degree murder without premeditation. 

His funeral was arranged, and a lot of fans participated in his funeral. A private funeral was also arranged where famous rappers were seen. He was entombed in a Mausoleum at Gardens of Boca Raton Memorial Park, Boca Raton, Florida, the U.S.
