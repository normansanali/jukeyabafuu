Career

Candice King started her career as a singer in the year 2006. She released her debut album titled It’s Always the Innocent Ones. After that, she co-wrote 12 out of 14 records like Voice Carry and Our Breakup Song.

In the year 2008, her debut album was again released and this time in Japan, and gained huge popularity and success. After that, she did various tours as a backup singer with Miley Cyrus. Along with her singing career, she also worked as an actress and made her appearance in various television series and movies.

She has worked in films like Pirate Camp, Juno, On the Doll, Love Hurts, Splitting the Bill, After We Collided, and many other movies. She also worked in much popular television series like How I Met Your Mother, Supernatural, The Originals, The Orville, When I Was 17, Her Own Worst Enemy, Fashion Police, Howling at the Moon, and various other TV series.

She also hosts the Directionally Challenged podcast along with her co-star and co-creator Kayla Ewell. She has won the Teen Choice Awards twice in her career for Scene Stealer Female.
