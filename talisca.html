Talisca Age, Biography

Talisca, also known as Anderson Souza Conceiço, was born on February 1, 1994. The Brazilian footballer is from Feira de Santana. Talisca lived with his father until he was 11 years old, then with his grandmother, and finally with his mother. Despite these adversities, Talisca remained devoted to football and began playing for the Feira de Santana Children’s Support Foundation (FAMFS), where he earned the nickname “Doda.” In 2023, he celebrated his 29th birthday.

His childhood experiences had a significant impact on his life, which he later described as the most difficult period of his life. However, he never gave up on his dream of playing football and worked tirelessly to improve his skills. His hard work eventually paid off, and he became a professional football player, playing for teams such as Esporte Clube Bahia, S.L. Benfica, and Guangzhou Evergrande Taobao. His rise to prominence in the football world is a testament to his tenacity and fortitude.
