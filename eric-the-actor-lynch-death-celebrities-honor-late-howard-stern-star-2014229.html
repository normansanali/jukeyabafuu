Eric the Actor Lynch Death: Celebrities Honor Late Howard Stern Star

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 18, 2024

“I loved Eric, I truly, truly loved him..he was the greatest…” @HowardStern on #EricTheActor pic.twitter.com/vlLDEtGse4

— Stern Show (@sternshow) September 22, 2014

A legacy remembered. Eric "The Actor" Lynch passed away this past weekend at the age of 39. The Howard Stern Show personality was honored following his death by celebrities and his colleagues. 

Stars took to social media after the news broke with outpourings of grief and remembrances for Lynch. The late actor was rushed to a Sacramento hospital on Saturday, Sept. 20, after suffering from cardiac problems, TMZ reported. 

Howard Stern, who worked with Eric the Actor for years, remembered his late friend on his radio show on Monday, Sept. 22. "I loved Eric," he said. "I truly, truly loved him… he was the greatest." 

Below, other tributes to Lynch. 

Zach Braff tweeted, "RIP ?#EricTheActor?. If there's an after-life I hope you're bigger than Johnny Depp."

Jimmy Kimmel wrote, "Eric the Actor was my all-time favorite @HowardStern caller and I will miss him terribly #ByeForNow"
