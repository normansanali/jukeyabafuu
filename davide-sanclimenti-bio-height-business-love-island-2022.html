Meet Love Island's Davide Sanclimenti! His Bio, Height, Business, Parents

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 23, 2024

Meet Davide Sanclimenti ready to claim that £50,000 and a partner he could spend his life with.

This article covers his age, height, business, and his journey so far in Love Island season 8. Delve into this Davide Sanclimenti Bio to unfold more information.

Davide Sanclimenti On Love Island 2022

Davide Sanclimenti joined Love Island 2022 because he believed it was going to be “so much fun”. He shared, “I’m going to be the Italian stallion in the Villa! With my Italian charm, I can also be a very romantic guy”.

The Italian handsome hunk added, “I’m good looking and I’ve realized since I first arrived in Manchester, a lot of English girls actually love me. They love to be around me and I love to be around them. It’s going to be fun, I’m looking forward to it”.

Davide has a decent motive when it comes to finding a partner. He came looking for “the one”, a soulmate with whom he could build something in the future, grow with them, and be a family.

Upon entering the show to shake things up, Davide grabbed the attention of Gemma Owen, daughter of former football star Michael Owen. When she chose to kiss Davide and revealed her age, it created tension among the viewers regarding their age difference of 8 years. Some viewers even threatened to write to Ofcom if they ever coupled up.

Indiyah also commented on Davide’s arrival saying, “When I saw him, I was very confused because I had no warning but he is very good looking!” Tasha gushed: “Straight away I was like, ‘Wow. Fit. Hot. Sexy. Hercules’”. Paige added: “I’m done. I’m ready to get married. I’m ready to have babies”.

Likewise, Davide was also thoroughly impressed by the girls on the show. The business owner said: “When I walked into the villa, I saw all the girls and I’m really impressed, I’m not going to lie”.

But this is all set to change, as Davide learned at the end of last night’s episode that he would pick a girl to couple up with.

Is Davide Sanclimenti Dating Anyone Today?

Davide Sanclimenti is mum about his current relationship status. He was asked about why he was single at the time of joining the show, Davide replied that he was “picky” and saw himself as a “relationship kind of guy”. He added, “I prefer to be in a relationship but at the same time I don’t do a lot of dates”.

Listing the “icks”, Davide listed, “If a girl is really rude, or if she’s not giving me enough attention, I will just lose interest very quickly… If I receive nothing back, no feedback or appreciation, I will lose interest in that person”.

Speaking to Paige, Davide complimented: “I fall in love for the personality and if you have a good heart. You are a beautiful girl – you’re a good looking girl”. So, it was still unclear who he was and who he coupled with.

Davide Sanclimenti Age

In 2022, Davide Sanclimenti is 27 years old.

Davide Sanclimenti Parents

Coming to details about his parents, Davide Sanclimenti hasn’t shared any. He doesn’t follow any of his family members on his Instagram as well as Facebook. The only thing that is remotely related to his family which he shared was they describe him as “a very deep person,” with a “good heart.”

He said: “For the people around me, I’m always there, ready to support and help them in a moment of need.”

Davide Sanclimenti Business

Per LinkedIn, Davide Sanclimenti serves as the director of S Deluxe Shisha. He has been serving in the business since July 2021 in Manchester Area. For five months from February to June 2021, Davide served as Junior Financial Officer for Phillips Trust Corporation Ltd.

Before that, he had worked for Bridgewater Finance Group as an accounts assistant from January to November 2020.

Davide started his career as a sales specialist and later became sales manager at Libertine Club in 2019.

Davide received his Bachelor’s Degree in Economics and Business Administration from Università degli Studi dell’Aquila. In 2019, he secured his Msc in accounting and finance from The University of Salford. Moreover, he also earned his degree in Finance & Banking from the University of Rome Tor Vergata.
