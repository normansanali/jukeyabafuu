Tony Lopez: TikTok Star, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth !

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Tony Lopez is a TikTok star and dancer. Another reason for his popularity is being the brother of the famous TikTok star Ondreaz Lopez. Furthermore, Tony’s TikTok account has more than 22M followers, where he regularly posts videos featuring choreographed dance performances, lip sync videos, and short comedies. 

Tony and Ondreaz have collaborated and run a YouTube channel, Lopez Brothers, one of the famous YouTube duos. Here they post challenges and dance videos. 

In late 2019, Tony became a member of The Hype House’s social group alongside his brother, Chase Hudson and Avani Gregg. Further, in July 2019, he hosted a Dance Workshop in Las Vegas. 

Tony’s birth date is 19th August 1999. He was born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. Currently, he is 23. His ethnicity is Hispanic, as his father is Cuban, and his mother is of English, German, and Scottish descent.

While Tony was in high school, he and his elder brother started performing short shows and crowd stunts. Later, they opened their dance workshop, where they taught dance to beginners. Both brothers live in LA, whereas their family, including their brother, still resides in Las Vegas. 

Tony Lopez Family Background: 

As per Tony’s Instagram profile, his mother’s name is Michelle Lopez. In May 2020, he featured his mom in one of his TikTok videos. Further, Tony has two brothers in siblings named Ondreaz and Xavier. 

Tony Lopez Education:

Tony attended a high school in Nevada. He and Ondreaz used to participate in school activities during their school days. 

Tony Lopez Physical stats:

Tony stands 5 feet and 8 inches tall and weighs around 70kg. His curly dark brown hair and attractive light brown eyes make him look more handsome. Tony has a lean muscular frame, and he is a lookout guy popular for his sweet smile. He always wears Christ sign earrings. Further, he is a tattoo lover and has numerous tattoos on his arms.

Tony Lopez Net worth:

Tony’s primary source of income is as a social media influencer. Additionally, he earns from endorsing various brands, his dance workshop, and his social media sponsorship. Tony estimated networth is around $1 million.

Tony Lopez Relationship Status: 

Currently, Tony is single. In 2021, he was married to influencer Sarah-Jade Bleau. The couple celebrated their six-month anniversary.

Previously Tony was linked to Sofie Dossi and Nikita Dragun. 

Tony Lopez Career:

Tony came to fame as a dancer in July 2019 when he hosted a Dance Workshop in Nevada. The same year he also became a member of The Hype House, a collective of TikTok personalities, which boosted his popularity. Tony collaborated with other house members, including his brother Ondreaz and created several videos. With his outstanding dancing skills, Tony’s TikTok account started earning millions of views and followers. Currently, his TikTok account has over 23M followers.  

Tony’s TikTok account gathered fans and helped him gain followers on other social media platforms like Instagram and Twitter. His Instagram page, where he posts exciting pictures about his personal and professional life, has gathered more than 6M followers. His Twitter account, created in July 2011, has accumulated more than 460k followers.

Further, Tony is also a YouTuber. He runs a YouTube channel named Lopez Brothers. He posts various kinds of videos, such as challenges and storytime videos, which his fans and subscribers often appreciate. The YouTube channel, created on 29th October 2019, currently has more than 1.54 million subscribers.

Tony has also used his fame to make his merchandise. He is multi-talented and does models to sell his merchandise, including T-shirts and hoodies.

Tony Lopez Social Appearance:

On TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter, Tony has more than 22M, 4.8M, and 417K followers, respectively. Further, on Youtube, he has 1.55M subscribers. Tony is active on all his social media profiles and regularly uploads and posts to keep his fans updated.

Tony Lopez is one of the famous names among the TikTok stars. His charming personality, dancing skills, and engaging content are the main reasons for his massive fan following. Our warm wishes are always with Tony.
