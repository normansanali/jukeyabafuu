Princess Diana's Astrologer Says Prince Louis' Behavior Was Predicted

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Nobody can predict how kids will act — except, apparently, the stars. Princess Diana’s astrologer opened up about how Kate Middleton and Prince William’s son Prince Louis’s “playful, cheeky” behavior can be explained by astrology, and it’s fascinating!

Celebrity astrologer Debbie Frank told Hello! Magazine that Louis, who turns 6 tomorrow, is a “breath of fresh air” who “provides some light relief from the stiff and staid royal line-up.” That’s something we’ve definitely seen over the years! “He’s so engaging, playful, cheeky, and utterly himself that people fall in love with his court jester antics,” she told the outlet.  He is guaranteed to put a smile on everyone’s faces, even though he is openly seen to remonstrate with his parents at royal events.”

Diving into his star charts, Frank can explain how the stars influence his behavior (because apparently it’s more than just being the baby of the family!). “His is an affectionate and loving personality with Venus in Taurus, Cancer rising, and the Moon in Leo,” Frank said. “He is a crowd pleaser even though he breaks the royal mold. Adorable and engaging, he is easily forgiven, and his attractive personality magnetizes a lot of attention.”

And even though the British royal family are staunch supporters of following order and tradition, Prince Louis stands out.

“Louis is born to be his own person,” Frank continued. “His birth chart doesn’t share the astrological blueprint usually associated with the steadfast, sensible sign of Taurus. Louis’s Taurus qualities are overlit by Uranus, the wild child planet, which was positioned close to his Sun when he was born.” Wild child planet? Sounds like it was named after Prince Louis!  Related story A Source Revealed Where Angelina Jolie’s Focus Lies Amid Ongoing Feud With Ex Brad Pitt

“This means that he is disinclined to follow the herd (in this case royal protocol). He is likely to shake things up, add some mischief and disruption into the mix,” Frank added. These personality traits, and the fact that Louis has a “determined Mars in Capricorn,” can cause some tension between him and his mother.

“He is the one person who can make her lose her cool! Kate is very self-disciplined and taming her little tiger cub is a challenge for her.” No doubt even more so now that Middleton is going through cancer treatment!

Earlier this month, former BBC royal correspondent Jennie Bond predicted what Prince Louis’s future will look like as the royal “spare.” (His older brother Prince George, 10, is set to inherit the throne one day, with his older sister Princess Charlotte, 8, next in line.)

“I imagine they will encourage Louis to explore life outside the royal fold … it could be the military, but it could also be working in the charity world or whatever he finds appealing after his education is finished,” she said. “I’m sure they will encourage him to go to University … And from there they will want him to find a life that is meaningful to him as well as appropriate for the son of a future King.”

“They will try to ensure that he feels he is living a life of value, irrespective of his place in the line of succession and that will probably involve service of some kind as they have emphasized from the start that they want their children to understand that having empathy with others is not only a kindness, but is rewarding as well,” she added.

And until the stars and planets decide to reveal what Prince Louis’s future holds, we’ll just have to wait and see.
