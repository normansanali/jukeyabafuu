LeBron James is an American professional basketball player. LeBron James plays for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association NBA. Likewise, the basketballer has gained massive fame and followers over the years and has become a Basketball and social media icon.

Early Life

LeBron James was born in the year 1984 and celebrates his birthday on December 30 every year. He was born in Akron, Ohio, USA. His full name is LeBron Raymone James Sr. Likewise, the professional basketball player is 39 years old at the moment. He belongs to American nationality and was born under the star sign Capricorn.

Education
