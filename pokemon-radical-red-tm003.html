TM003 Flip Turn in Pokmon Radical Red FandomSpot

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 5, 2024
LocationReward after defeating Misty in the Cerulean City Gym.
DescriptionAfter attacking, the user switches places with a party Pokémon.
TypeWater

To get TM03 you have to defeat Misty at the Cerulean City gym.

It’s a mandatory battle, and there are no prior requirements you have to complete to challenge her. Simply walk into the building next to the Cerulean City Pokémon Center and challenge her.
