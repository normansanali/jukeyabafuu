Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Massey Ferguson 235 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers,  Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

The Massey Ferguson 135 was the first of the Massey Ferguson 100 range, and was a success to the Massey Ferguson 35. Production started in 1964 and ended in 1975, when it was succeeded by the Massey Ferguson 235.

Massey Ferguson 235 Utility Tractor was equipped with a Perkins AD3.152 diesel 3-cylinder liquid-cooled 152 ci [2.5 L] engine, which delivers 35 hp [26.1 kW] Drawbar (claimed) power at rated rpm 2250.

Massey Ferguson 235 Utility Tractor had a Perkins 2.5L 3-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, differential mechanical expanding shoe brakes, 3830 to 4135 pounds weight, and 6 qts [5.7 L] engine oil capacity.
