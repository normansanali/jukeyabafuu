Following that, Carlos was named to the world roster for the All-Star Future Games in 2006, 2007, and 2008.

In September 2009, he made his debut. From 2009 to 2011, he played a lot of games and did a lot of good work.

Carrasco resumed playing a year later; however, his performance had deteriorated significantly.
