Meet Muriel Montrose: Mother Of Tony Dow

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 22, 2024

American film star, producer, director and sculptor, Tony Dow was born on April 13th 1945 in the Hollywood neighborhood of Los Angeles, California in the United States of America.

Tony Dow, born Anthony Lee Dow happens to be the son of Muriel Montrose, a stuntwoman in westerns, and John Stevens Dow, a designer, and contractor.

Who is Muriel Montrose?

Muriel Montrose was a stuntwoman in westerns, and John Stevens Dow, a designer, and contractor. She is also the mother of Tony Dow.

She was born on May 27th, 1906 in Denver, Colorado in the United States of America. She died on April 30th, 2001 in Monterey, California in the United States of America.

Muriel Montrose net worth

Muriel’s net worth before her death has not been documented on the web yet.

Muriel Montrose age

Muriel was 95 years before she died in 2001, 30th April.

Muriel Montrose family

Muriel’s family moved to  California when she was a teenager. In 1927 she married Ray Lloyd Ramsey but they were divorced soon after. She married John Stephen Dow, a designer and general contractor, on February 11, 1929. They moved to the San Fernando Valley and had two sons. Her younger son Tony Dow became an actor and starred in the 1950s television series Leave It To Beaver.
