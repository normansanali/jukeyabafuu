Irina Shayks Sexy Mirror Selfie Included a Daringly Low-Rise Micro-Miniskirt And a Tiny Chain Bra

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 11, 2024

If one thing's for certain, it's that Irina Shayk isn’t afraid to stray away from expected attire (see: naked dresses and pantless looks). But just when we thought her street style couldn’t get any more intriguing, she once again proved that she could pull off anything while wearing almost nothing at all.

On Tuesday, the supermodel debuted the confidence-boosting look in question when detailing an OOTD on her Instagram Story. In the pic, Irina sported nothing but a barely-there triangle-shaped Gucci chain bra teamed with a daringly low-rise yellow leather micro mini-skirt (which featured exposed white pockets). The A-lister layered the look underneath a black leather oversized trench coat to combat dipping temperatures, and she finished the 'fit with a pair of black platform pumps.

This isn't the first time Irina has taken the less is more trend for a spin. Just last month, the model proved she isn't a regular mom — she's a cool mom — when modeling morning school drop-off fashion.

Spotted walking her and Bradley Cooper's 6-year-old daughter, Lea De Seine, to school, she left her pants at home and opted for a black satin button-down layered with an oversized dark blue blazer. A black Hermès handbag, shield sunglasses, white wireless headphones, and a pair of black Mary Jane heels provided the finishing touches to her no-pants look. As for her glam, she kept it simple, pairing her dewy skin with a pink pout and styling her long brown hair in a sleek, braided ponytail.
