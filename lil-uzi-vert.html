Uzi featured on the hip-hop trio Migos’ single “Bad and Boujee”. The single was released on August 31, 2016, and is from the trio’s second studio album, Culture (2017). In January 2017, around the time the album released, the single debuted atop the US Billboard Hot 100, becoming Uzi’s first number 1 single as a lead or featured artist, and his highest charting single as both. On February 27, 2017, Woods released the EP Luv Is Rage 1.5 whilst on tour with The Weeknd. The EP was positively received for its “nerdy” nature with Kingdom Hearts themed instrumentals and lyrics referring to anime, Steven Universe and hentai. The EP was also credited as the start of Woods’ affiliation with the “emo-rapper” label due to the heartbreak-associated lyrics on the song “Luv Scars K.o 1600” and references to threats of suicide and depression on the stand-out track “XO Tour Llif3”.
