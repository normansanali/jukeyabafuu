Costa Titch: Discover Everything About The Rappers Relationships, Music And More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 8, 2024

Costa Titch whose real name is Costa Tsobanglou is a popular super talented rapper, dancer, and songwriter in South Africa who has won the 2X SAHA2020 Award.

Costa Titch was born in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga, in 1995 september 10. He is a super talented rapper and songwriter in South Africa and he is also known for his hit songs like Nkalakatha and Activate.

The rapper, who was a dancer before, has been able to create a style for himself that everybody can recognize. Initially, he has been dropping songs continuously since his debut. 

The rapper’s achievements weren’t seen at first but his love for music and consistency makes him the superstar he is today. As well as he won the hearts of many people with his song Nkalakatha.

Costa already had a successful career as a dancer with the New age Steez crew when he decided to set his vision on musical success. Moreover many have been confused about the rapper’s race, but he is a white boy from Nelspruit, However, he had a lot of colored friends in the music industry.

Who Is Costa Titch?

South African super talented rapper, Costa Titch spent his early childhood at Nelspruit, Mpumalanga.

The South American dancer, rapper, and songwriter is a 26 years old talented rap artist. Furthermore, his hit song Activate, helps him to grab public attention and has increased his fan base over time. His song Activate became a big hit after its release due to his line Isizulu in his performance.

After receiving a positive response from his fans, Costa decided to take his rap skill to next level. His style, which is an infusion of indigenous trap music has increased his followership in south Africa.

Costa has become a successful rapper with significant acceptance in the entertainment industry. When he developed an interest in entertainment he was younger, he chose the dance path and started following it as a hobby.

Costa Titch began his professional dancing after his move to Johannesburg. He joined Tumi Tladi and Phantom Steeze to perform dance sessions as a crew (New Age Steez dance crew). They took part in the Hip Hop International Dance Competition and achieve the 14th position in the competition.
