Theory Aspyn-Sky Gray Age: How Old Is John Grays Daughter?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 13, 2024

Theory Aspyn-Sky, the daughter of assistant pastor of Lakewood Church, is 9 years old.

Theory has a brother named John W. Gray IV.

John and his sister do not have any of their personal information available on the media space.

Who Is John W. Gray IV’s Father Pastor John Gray?

Pastor John Gray is an American pastor who assists at Lakewood Church.

He doubles as a gospel singer, and television personality, known for appearing on the Fox TV show, The Preachers.

Pastor Gray on his own hosts the television program John Gray World.

He launched his TV ministryin the mid-1990s and at the time, he was also serving as backup singer for American gospel artiste Kirk Franklin.

Pastor Gray has also been an associate pastor at Joel Osteen’s Lakewood Church in Houston.

The TV program “The Book of John Gray”, which airs on the Oprah Winfrey Network, reilves around him. The program is currently in its 4th season.

Pastor Gray is married to his wife, Aventer Gray, who is an avid supporter of his ministry and a lover of Christ.
