Karan Boolani Biography

Karan Boolani was born on October 6, 1982, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He is 41 years old as of 2023

Karan Boolani received his high school education at Mahindra United World College of India, according to his Facebook page. For his post-graduate studies, he enrolled at Bond University in Australia. He finished his Master of Arts in Film and Television.

Karan Boolani is the eldest child of Vijay and Binu Boolani (mother). Vijay Boolani, his father, is a well-known engineer and entrepreneur. He founded and owns ‘Boolani Engineering Corporation.’

Binu Boolani is a housewife and the family’s rock. She looks after the house and cooks delicious meals for her husband and children. Karishma Boolani is Karan’s younger sister. Karishma works as a Sports Scientist and entrepreneur. Boolani adheres to Hinduism as a religion.

Karan Boolani Career

Karan Boolani is passionate about directing and producing films and commercials. According to his IMDB profile, he produced 500+ advertisements in over 38 countries prior to being accepted for post-graduate studies.

He began working as a full-time producer and director after finishing his studies. He has also worked with Pepsi, L’oreal, Google, Mastercard, Nike, HUL, and many other branded products.

In 2008, he made his film debut as an assistant director with the film ‘Saas Bahu Aur Sensex.’ The following year, he worked on ‘Wake Up Sid.’ Karan directed the popular TV series ’24’ in 2016 after gaining a lot of experience. He appeared in ten episodes. Actor Anil Kapoor played the lead role in the film.

He worked as a director and producer on the Netflix TV series ‘Selection Day’ from 2018 to 2019. It was one of his most laudable works. In 2020, he directed the short film ‘Lisa Mishra: Nai Chaida.’
