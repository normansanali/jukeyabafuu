Get To Know Robin Fierce! Her Real Name, Out Of Drag, Height

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Robin Fierce, a Connecticut-based entertainer, is on season 15 of RuPaul’s Drag Race to entertain the viewers. But many details related to Robin are still a mystery; such as her real name, out of drag, job, and partner.

We tackle these aspects of her life in this article below. So, keep scrolling down and learn all about Robin.

Robin Fierce On RuPaul’s Drag Race

Robin Fierce, the contestant in season 15 of RuPaul’s Drag Race, started drag when she was around 20 years old.

She told World Of Wonder, “It’s been about 6 years now! I’ve always been an artistic kid but always felt a little shame about queerness growing up. When I was 19, I started to come out and investigate queer culture. I found Drag Race and found that drag queens weren’t as weird as I thought or at least no more weird than I was lol. I started practicing makeup for like six months before stepping out into the world… GRANTED, she was still a little crunchy, but it was amazing… a confidence builder I didn’t know I needed. I loved that drag really brought all the art I’ve always loved in one place and the rest is her-story they say!”

Robin is the drag daughter of Mo Heart. This makes Luxx Noir London and Black Peppa, both competitors in Season 15 of RuPaul’s Drag Race UK, her drag sisters. When they participated in RuPaul’s Drag Race, Robin, Amethyst, and Loosey LaDuca were the first competitors who called Connecticut home

“Robin” is a homage to Season 2 and All Stars Season 1 runner-up Raven – Robin also wanted a bird for her drag name. “Fierce” comes for what her drag is “no matter what”.

She hails from a little-represented drag scene in the northeastern state, which she says boasts “camp, spooky, ooky, glamour” and “high-energy queens”. 2022 was the second year she has been doing drag full-time. Her artistry is the love child of Mariah Carey, Beyoncé, and Diahann Carroll. Robin has been described as a total package as she dances, sings, and hosts.

“I get my grandiose ladiness from [them],” Robin explains, citing the Dynasty star as a particular inspiration because she mixed her high-brow aura with comedic sass. “When I think diva, I don’t think diva, like, ‘Where’s my champagne, darling?’ It’s not in an aggressive way, but diva-like, ‘Hi, I’m here, we’re about to do a thing and get into it because the face is on, the body is on, the look is on,'” she elaborates. “I think you’ll get the divadom in the best way.”

Speaking about her live performances with Thotyssey, she said: “I mean, they range. I lip sync, I sing, I do high energy, ballads, and comedy. So it really depends on the day… but it’s always a good time.”

Robin Fierce Out Of Drag

What Is Robin Fierce Real Name?

The real name of Robin Fierce is Rashawn Lee.

Robin Fierce Age

Robin Fierce turned 27 years old in 2022. Her date of birth is 15 October 1995.

Robin Fierce Job

Robin Fierce previously worked at Best Buy. She had said that getting fired from the business during the time of COVID was the best thing that ever happened to her. She added that if she wasn’t fired she probably wouldn’t have left the job voluntarily to pursue full-time drag.

Previously, Best Buy had let her sell TV in drag. “I fully have sold TVs in drag. I’m dead serious,” the Connecticut queen told Entertainment Weekly, fondly recalling her former job as an electronics saleswoman, where customers watched her work a sales floor while their mouths hung “agape” as she hawked Toshibas in her “auntie bob” wig, full padding, and a fresh mug.

“Drag queens are nothing but innovative when it comes to doing our craft,” she told CT Insider in February 2021. Drag queens were able to shift their performance style to suit the pandemic relatively easily. Other performers and artistic endeavors might have had a difficult time coming to terms with the new pandemic normal, but drag queens were well situated from the start according to Robin.

According to her LinkedIn, Robin is currently working as a Freelance Makeup Artist at Anastasia Beverly Hills. She had worked as Head Creative at her business RahVisions. Other jobs of Robin include First Responder at KOTA, Red Zone Specialist & Family Room Specialist at Apple Inc., and Cashier at McDonald’s.

Robin has an undergraduate degree (BFA) in Film/Cinema/Video Studies from Full Sail University. She completed high school at Howell Cheney Technical High School.

How Tall Is Robin Fierce?

Robin Fierce’s height measures above 5’10”.
