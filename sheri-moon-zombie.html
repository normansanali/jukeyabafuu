Sheri Moon Zombie Biography

Sheri Moon Zombie is best known as Actress, Fashion Designer, Former dancer and model who has an estimated Net Worth of $5 Million. Leading horror actress who has starred in The Devil's Rejects, Halloween and Halloween II. In 2007, she played Eva in Grindhouse: Werewolf Women of the SS. She was born on September 26,1970, California. She had her first horror role in House of 1000 Corpses. She attended the Connecticut School of Broadcasting, but did not graduate. She co-starred in The Devil's Rejects with Sid Haig . She married director and musician Rob Zombie in 2002. Her zodiac sign is Libra.
