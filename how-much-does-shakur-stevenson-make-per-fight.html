How much does Shakur Stevenson make per fight?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Shakur Stevenson, also known as "The Fearless," is a prominent figure in the boxing world, celebrated for his accomplishments in the ring. His earnings from each fight are a topic of interest to many, given his significant impact in the sport. 

Fight Purses and Earnings

Stevenson's income primarily stems from his fight fees and sponsorships, with reported earnings averaging around 200,000 US Dollars.

While his income varies from fight to fight, he has garnered substantial amounts from his notable matches.

For instance, he earned approximately 1.7 million dollars from his fight with Jamel Herring, 1.5 million dollars against Jeremia Nakathila, 500,000 dollars against Toka Kahn Clary, and 450,000 dollars against Felix Caraballo.

It's estimated that Stevenson's purse for his lightweight debut against Shuichiro Yoshino was a guaranteed $1 million with an estimated 70% of the PPV shares, potentially resulting in a total of $3 million.

Endorsements and Sponsorships

Apart from fight purses, Stevenson's earnings are supplemented by endorsements and sponsorships, contributing significantly to his overall net worth. His association with various brands and companies has undoubtedly bolstered his financial standing in the sport.

Charitable Contributions and Foundations

Stevenson's contributions extend beyond the ring as he has actively engaged in charity work and established the Stevenson & Moses Boxing for Life Foundation with veteran boxer Wali Moses.

The foundation aims to promote boxing as a tool to instill discipline and ethics in young individuals, further demonstrating Stevenson's commitment to making a positive impact beyond his professional endeavors.

Net Worth and Lifestyle

Shakur Stevenson's net worth is estimated to be between 3 million dollars and 5 million dollars, primarily attributed to his successful boxing career and associated earnings. 

He also resides in a luxurious mansion in Virginia, underscoring his financial success and lifestyle.

Future Prospects and Earnings

Looking ahead, Stevenson's prospects for increasing his earnings remain promising.

His upcoming fights and potential unification bouts, such as the anticipated match against the winner of Devin Haney and Vasiliy Lomachenko, offer opportunities for substantial purses and enhanced financial rewards.

Shakur Stevenson's earnings from each fight encompass a combination of fight purses, endorsements, and charitable initiatives, solidifying his status as a financially successful and socially conscious athlete in the world of boxing.
