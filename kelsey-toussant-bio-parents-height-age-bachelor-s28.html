Kelsey Toussant Job

Kelsey Toussant is a model, actress, and realtor. She has been working as a realtor at Flower Street Group since August 2023. She is also the owner of a boutique named TOUSSANT. For two months in 2023, Kelsey also worked as a leasing agent at Cardinal Group Management.

Kelsey’s career began as head cashier at CARL’S JR. RESTAURANTS, LLC. She then became a California State University Northridge Caller at Ruffalocody LLC. From September 2011 to March 2013, she worked for Best Buy as a Mobile Sales Consultant.

From 2012 to 2013, Kelsey held several positions at California State University, Northridge. She worked as G.R.E.A.T Escape Summer Assistant, Community Center Assistant, and an an Academic Mentor. Kelsey and served as Personal Assistant to Creative Director and Brand Ambassador at From The Ground Up Productions {elevated trade show talent + event staffing}.

Apple also hired Kelsey as a Flagship Apple Specialist from 2013 to 2018. MGA Advertising her as Administrative Assistant. From 2018 to 2021, Kelsey worked as a Property Ambassador at Action Property Management and from March 2021 to June 2021 as Marketing Coordinator at Coldwell Banke.

Per her IMDB, Kelsey has worked on shows like Barbie, How I Met Your Father, The Heights, Send Help, Made for Love, Petty Dudes, and The Ride to name a few. She received her acting training at AMAW and Killian’s Workshop.

Kelsey is a graduate of California State University, Northridge.
