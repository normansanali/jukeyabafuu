Edebiri is well-known for her appearances in The Bear (2022–present) and Abbott Elementary (2023–present), as well as her voice work as Missy on Big Mouth (2020–present). Is Ayo Edebiri Dating Someone on the Show?

Only in “The Bear,” the TV show in which Ayo Edebiri appears as Sydney, does she have a partner. Actor Jeremy Allan White, who plays Chef Carmy, is her on-screen love interest. The show’s creators didn’t want to introduce love tensions between the two main characters at first.

She said in an interview that neither she nor White had given the idea much thought during production. But she said it was fun to see a different type of connection played out on TV. She also mentioned that it was nice to see a platonic connection between two men and two women on screen for a change.
