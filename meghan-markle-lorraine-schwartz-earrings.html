Meghan Markle Wore Updated Lorraine Schwartz Earrings to Lion King Premiere

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024
  • Meghan Markle wore newly-customized Lorraine Schwartz diamond earrings to The Lion King premiere in London on Sunday, July 14.
  • The earrings are a personal favorite of the duchess, who last wore them as simple diamond studs to Trooping the Colour last month.
  • Schwartz re-set the earrings for Meghan and added removable onyx jackets.

The Duchess of Sussex has long had a keen eye for accessories, and now, she's given one of her favorite pair of earrings a trendy update. While attending the European premiere of the live-action remake of The Lion King in London on Sunday, July 14, Duchess Meghan showed off her favorite pair of Lorraine Schwartz diamond studs, which the jeweler freshly updated with removable onyx jackets.

The duchess last wore the emerald-cut earrings at June's Trooping the Colour celebration, when the jewels were just diamond studs. Now, Meghan can customize the earrings depending on her mood.

Samir Hussein//Getty Images

The Duchess of Sussex attends the 2019 Trooping the Colour.

Sunday famously marked the first meeting between the Duchess of Sussex and Beyoncé, who voices Nala in the new Lion King. The two hugged and expressed their admiration for each other—fans even caught the singer mouthing “my princess” as she embraced Meghan!

WPA Pool//Getty Images

The Duchess of Sussex and Beyoncé meet at the European premiere of The Lion King on Sunday, July 14 in London.

Queen Bey also attended the event decked out in Lorraine Schwartz diamonds, pairing the jeweler's earrings and rings with a glorious custom Cong Tri gown.

David M. Benett//Getty Images

Beyoncé attends the European premiere of The Lion King on July 14, 2019.

This isn't the first time the Sussexes tapped Schwartz's design skills. For his first wedding anniversary, Prince Harry collaborated with the jewelry designer to build an eternity ring for the Duchess of Sussex. The ring features an emerald, sapphire, and peridot stone—the birthstones of Harry, Meghan, and Archie. Harry also had Schwartz resize and reset Meghan’s engagement ring with a new diamond band.
