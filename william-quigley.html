Biography

William Quigley was born in Pennsylvania on April 29, 1961. American artist whose primary medium is painting, but has also worked in fashion and film. He’s been active since the mid-80s and his work has been collected by Bill Clinton, Arnold Schwarzenegger, and Eminem, to name a few. Jackie Robinson, Ray Charles, Ethan Hawke, and Abraham Lincoln have all been subjects of his portraiture. From 1980-85 he attended Philadelphia College of Art as well as spending a year abroad at the Tyler School of Art in Rome. He went on to study Political Science at the University of Pennsylvania. Immediately after graduating from the Philadelphia College of Art, now University of the Arts, he made his first exhibition opening for Andy Warhol in 1985 at the Henry S. McNeil Gallery.
