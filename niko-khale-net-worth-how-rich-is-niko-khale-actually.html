Niko Khale Net Worth: How Rich is Niko Khale Actually?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Niko Khale is an American singer and songwriter from Charlotte, North Carolina. He is best known as popular artist Keyshia Cole’s boyfriend. As of 2020, Niko Khale net worth is estimated to be around $1.5 million.

Early Life

Niko Khale was born Niko K. Hale on October 17, 1995, in Washington, D.C., and later moved to Charlotte, North Carolina. In 2005, young Niko was posting music on his MySpace page. Khale used the stage name Ace Primo at first and changed it to his real name in 2019. His music caught the attention of producer Rodney Jerkins, who helped him start his hip-hop career.

Niko met his girlfriend Keyshia Cole at a mutual friend’s birthday party in 2018. The pair got into a relationship quickly thereafter, and they currently have one child together. They have made headlines due to their controversial 14-year age difference.

Career

Niko Khale’s career began around 2008, when he released freestyles and remixes on MySpace that caught the attention of producer Rodney “Darkchild” Jenkins. Khale launched his career from there, and has since released a number of songs.

Some of his popular hits include “Something Changed About You” and “Truth Is.” He came into the limelight with the announcement of his relationship with superstar Keyshia Cole in 2018. In late 2018, Khale released the album On My Own, which featured the songs “War Ready” and “Water Under The Bridge.”

Additionally, he was recently featured on Nelly Furtado’s album The Spirit Indestructible on the song “High Life.” In early 2020, Niko released his most recent album titled Yo Eye, which has mostly favorable reviews. He has no immediate plans to go on tour with his music, but appears to be focusing on his family at this time.

Niko has also been featured on Powerade, T-Mobile, and Hyundai commercials.

Niko Khale Net Worth

As of 2020, Niko Khale has a net worth of around $1.5 million or more. While he is best known for being Keyshia Cole’s boyfriend, he is certainly making a name for himself in the hip-hop world. He boasts 5.6k subscribers on YouTube.

His girlfriend Keyshia Cole is said to have a net worth of almost $10 million.
