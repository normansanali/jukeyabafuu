The Stylish Heir: Nats Getty Net Worth and Legacy

When you think of a true-blue Getty, you must surely have Nats Getty in mind. Nats is a respected artist, model, and philanthropist, known for his gothic and edgy style. But beyond his passions, what earned him respect and fame is his immense net worth and contribution to our society. Let’s get to know more about Nats Getty in this article.

Net Worth and Salary

Nats Getty’s net worth as of 2021 is $35 million, making him one of the wealthiest Getty heirs. With a wealth that most people can only dream of, Nats Getty’s net worth comes from his successful ventures in the fashion and entertainment industries.

Early Life and Education

Born in November 1992, Nats Getty is the grandson of John Paul Getty, the founder of Getty Oil, a corporate giant back in the 70s and 80s. Nats was raised in the UK and later attended Duke University in the USA where he studied in the political science program.

Career

True to his creative genes, Nats Getty started his career in fashion by launching his clothing line named Strike Oil. His brand has become popular for its androgynous, gothic, and punk-rock designs. Nats had also modeled for famous clothing brands such as Calvin Klein and Tommy Hilfiger.

As an artist, Nats Getty has a unique vision that translates well into his artwork. His art is regarded as a combination of punk-rock, glamour, and rebellion, which have been featured in galleries in Los Angeles and New York.

Personal Life

Nats Getty is married to Gigi Gorgeous, a renowned YouTuber and model. The couple tied the knot in 2019 in a lavish ceremony in Montecito, California.

Illness and Death

There is no illness or death to report on Nats Getty. Though there have been rumors about his health, Nats has been active on social media, posting updates about his art, fashion, and personal life, showing no signs of illness.

Real Estate

Nats Getty has a beautiful property in Los Angeles, California, which he shares with his wife Gigi Gorgeous. The property is valued at $8.1 million dollars and features Spanish-style architecture, a guest house, and a beautiful pool.

Legacy

Nats Getty’s legacy is not all about his wealth and popularity. His contribution to society has been notable, with his charitable activities supporting the LGBT community. Nats has sponsored many LGBTQ organizations such as the Trevor Project, which is a suicide prevention program for LGBTQ youths.
