Jennifer Lopez May Have Suffered a Nip Slip at the 2015 Golden Globes

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Let’s get to the bottom of this! 

Jennifer Lopez‘s cleavage-baring Zuhair Murad gown was the talk of the town (and the Internet) at the 2015 Golden Globes on Sunday, Jan. 11, and for more than its intricate Art Deco design and dazzling sequins. 

With all eyes on her ample assets (with a plunging neckline that dangerously low, can you blame Us?), a mysterious dark shading along her left breast appeared, which could have signaled a nip slip. 

Before calling it, though, let us remind you that this is the same woman who confidently wore a green scarf as a dress to the 2000 Grammy Awards with no wardrobe malfunction in sight.
