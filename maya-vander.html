    Maya Vander Facts

  • She was brought up in Israel.
  • In 2002, Maya relocated to the United States to live in Los Angeles.
  • She started off her career as a real estate agent with Nourmand & Associates in May 2011. From there, she went on to work for various other real estate companies including Keller Williams Realty, Inc., The Oppenheim Group, and Douglas Elliman Real Estate.
  • In December 2020, she founded the Maya Vander Group in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.
  • Besides Maya, other members of the cast of Selling Sunset cast are Chrishell Stause, Christine Quinn, Mary Fitzgerald, Heather Young, Amanza Smith, Maya Vander, Davina Potratz, Romain Bonnet, Jason Oppenheim, and Brett Oppenheim.
  • In May 2022, Maya announced the launch of her real estate firm, The Maya Vander Group.
