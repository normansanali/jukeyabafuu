Tom Bergeron Fired From 'Dancing With The Stars'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Tom Bergeron got fired from Dancing With The Stars (DWTS), which has come as a surprise. However, Bergeron had already opened about his departure from the franchise reality show on 14 July 2020 through Twitter. He shared a tweet a couple of months back in July and mentioned that Dancing With The Stars will resume without him.

'Just informed @DancingABC would be continuing without me. It's been an incredible 15-year run and the most unexpected gift of my career. I'm grateful for that and for the lifelong friendships made. That said, now what am I supposed to do with all of these glitter masks?' he wrote on his Twitter.

(©: Twitter/TomBergeron)

Concerning the reason behind firing tom Bergeron and Erin Andrews, ABC Network mentioned that the channel moving in a "new creative direction," and they wanted to change a few things up that might help revamp the show's popularity. It was all for attracting the viewers, as DWTS garnered the least viewers of 7.8 million last fall season.

Who Is Tom Bergeron's Wife?

Tom Bergeron, age 65, is married to his longtime partner, Lois Bergeron. The Bergeron couple tied a knot on 22 May 1982 and have been together for thirty-eight years. In the course of their nearly four decades of married life, the lovebirds have welcomed a couple of children, namely, Samantha Bergeron and Jessica Bergeron.

Tom Bergeron and his daughters (©: Instagram/Tom Bergeron)

However, Tom Bergeron's wife has been staying out of the limelight since they are connected. Since the lady Bergeron leads a low-key lifestyle, there is not much information regarding her family life and her bond with the former Dancing With The Stars host.

Net Worth and Salary Details Of Tom Bergeron

Tom Bergeron, an American television host, has a net worth of $16 million and also cashes out the salary of $150 Thousand per episode. He is widely known as the TV host of Hollywood Squares, America's Funniest Home Videos, and Who Wants to Be a Millionaire.

Besides that, his credits include movies and tv shows like People Are Talking (1987-90), MADtv (2003-04), Square Off (2006), Who Wants to Be a Millionaire (2010), Who Do You Think You Are? (2015), The View (2001-17), Celebrity Page (2019), Entertainment Tonight (2005-20), and many others.
