Who is Adam Schefter?

Adam Schefter is known as an American sportswriter. He is known as one of the good editors at The Michigan Daily and he has appeared in a movie named The Longest Yard.

In 2005. He wrote many articles for Rocky Mountain News and Denver Post in September 1990 and July 1996.

Adam Schefter’s Early life, Childhood, and Education

Being an American sportswriter he has a good attraction for his studies. He was born on 1966 December at 21Valley Stream, New York, but he spent his childhood in  Bellmore, New York.

He belongs to American nationality and Jewish ethnicity. There is no information on his parents and siblings.

His education, in  June 1985, he graduated from John F. Kennedy High School, and in 1989 graduated from the University of Michigan.

Finally, he made his study life successful when he graduated from Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism.

Adam Schefter’s Career, Salary, and Net Worth

Being a successful  American sportswriter he began his career as an editor at The Michigan Daily.

Then in September 1990, he started writing for the Rocky Mountain News and also the Denver Post in July 1996 when he was hired by the Denver Post.

After his writing skills were seen, he was working as a part of  ESPN’s “The Sports Reporters”. He then started a Woody Paige on ESPN’s show, “Around the Horn”.

After all, he joined the NFL Network in 2004 and he was also a sideline reporter for NBC occasionally.

After all the hard work as a writer which he has shown finally in 2009, he was the most popular sportswriter and also an analyst in the United States, and ESPN quickly grabbed him from the NFL Network for the betterment of his career.

Now he currently works for both ESPN radio and ESPN. His net worth is $1.2 million and his salary is $4 million per year.
