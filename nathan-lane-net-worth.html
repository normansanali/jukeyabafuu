What is Nathan Lane’s Net Worth and Salary?

Nathan Lane is an American actor and comedian who has a net worth of $18 million. Throughout his long and successful career, he has earned a considerable amount of money from his work in theater, film, and television. As one of the most recognizable faces in entertainment, Nathan Lane is highly respected among his peers and beloved by his fans.

Early Life and Education

Nathan Lane was born Joseph Lane in Jersey City, New Jersey, on February 3, 1956. His father, Daniel, was a truck driver, and his mother, Nora, was a housewife. Growing up, Nathan Lane was drawn to the theater and often played the lead role in school plays.

After graduating from high school, Nathan Lane attended St. Joseph’s College in Philadelphia but dropped out after just one semester to pursue an acting career. He moved to New York City and started working in the theater industry, performing in off-Broadway productions.

Career

Nathan Lane’s breakthrough role came in 1982 when he played Nathan Detroit in the Broadway revival of “Guys and Dolls.” He went on to star in numerous Broadway productions, including “The Producers,” “A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to the Forum,” and “Angels in America.”

In addition to his work on stage, Nathan Lane has appeared in numerous films and television shows. Some of his most notable film roles include “The Birdcage,” “Mousehunt,” and “The Lion King,” in which he voiced the character of Timon. On television, he has had recurring roles in “Modern Family,” “Penny Dreadful,” and “The Good Wife.”

Personal Life

Nathan Lane is a private person and has not revealed much about his personal life. However, he has been open about his sexuality and came out as gay in 1999. He has been in a long-term relationship with his partner, Devlin Elliott, since 1999, and the couple married in 2015.

Real Estate

Nathan Lane currently lives in a luxurious estate in the Hamptons, which he purchased in 2017 for $5.65 million. The property features breathtaking views of the ocean and includes a swimming pool and tennis court.

Legacy

Nathan Lane’s talent and contributions to the entertainment industry have made him one of the most respected and beloved actors of his generation. He has won numerous awards over the course of his career, including three Tony Awards, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Daytime Emmy Award.
